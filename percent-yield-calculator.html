Guide: Entering Values

Our calculators use a smartParser to handle scientific notation.

^
Exponent: 10^5 means 105
*
Multiply: 10*1 means 10 multiplied by 1.
e
Scientific: 1.8e-5 means 1.8 × 10-5

Practical Examples:

  • Positive: Enter 10^2 for 100.
  • Negative: Enter -5 for acidic values.
  • Complex: Enter 10^-7 for neutral pH.
Scientific Rules of Efficiency

1. Theoretical Yield: Absolute max mass predicted by Stoichiometry.
2. Enthalpy: Exothermic (ΔH < 0) vs Endothermic (ΔH > 0).
3. Balancing: Law of Conservation of Mass must be satisfied.

Scientific Research Context

In Green Chemistry, "Atom Economy" and "Percent Yield" are the primary indicators of a process's environmental impact. Researchers use mass balance equations to ensure that no toxic precursors are wasted, thereby reducing the environmental "E-factor" of pharmaceutical synthesis.
