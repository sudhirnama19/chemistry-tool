Guide: Entering Values

Our calculators use a smartParser to interpret scientific notation and standard math symbols.

^
Exponent: Use for powers. 10^1 means 10 to the power of 1.
*
Multiply: 10*1 means 10 multiplied by 1.
e
Scientific: 1.8e-5 is shorthand for 1.8 × 10⁻⁵.

Practical Examples:

  • Positive Exponents: Enter 10^2 for 100.
  • Negative Integers: Enter -5 for acidic calculations.
  • Complex Powers: Enter 10^-7 for neutral pH water.
