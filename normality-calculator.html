Normality ($N$) Definition

Normality is the number of gram equivalents of solute dissolved per liter of solution. Unlike molarity, it accounts for the reactive capacity (n-factor) of the species.

Core Formulas:
  • $N = Molarity \times n\text{-factor}$
  • $N = \frac{Weight(g)}{Eq. Weight \times Volume(L)}$
